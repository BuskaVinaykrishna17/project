About us

Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology (GCET) is a private technical institution located in Reddigudam, Andhrapradesh. The college was established in the year 2005 under the aegis of Teja Educational Society. It is an autonomous institution accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Being affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad, GCET strives to impart technical education with the latest advancements. Admission to the college is done on the basis on entrance examination namely EAMCET, ECET, ICET, and PGECET. .

The Institute aims at evolving strong research programmes in a plethora of areas where IT shall provide a common link with profound emphasis on development of technology and its application.
